FONTLOG for Khmer Digital

This file provides detailed information on the Khmer Digital font software.

-----------------------------------------------------------

2026-05-22 (Initial Release)

- First official public release of Khmer Digital font family
- Developed by Ministry of Post and Telecommunications of Cambodia (MPTC)
- Includes 9 weights: Thin to Black
- Full Khmer Unicode support with Latin compatibility
- Optimized for web, mobile, and desktop environments
- Tested within Microsoft 365 ecosystem (Word, Excel, Teams, SharePoint)
- Includes webfont versions (WOFF2) for modern browser usage
